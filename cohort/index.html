YOUR 3-DAY ROADMAP

Day 1: AI ART Matrix™ – Strategic Foundation

What you’ll learn: How to evaluate ANY business problem and determine if AI is the right solution.

The framework: AI ART Matrix (Alignment, Readiness, and Transformation)

  • 1 hour teaching + practice: We walk through the framework with live examples. You’ll see how to ask the right questions about your own business.
  • 1 hour group breakout: You’ll work in small groups on a real use case. We’ll refine your thinking in real time.
  • 30 min Q&A: Ask me anything about your specific problems. We’ll help you think through them.

By end of Day 1: You’ll have evaluated 3–5 of your own business problems and ranked them by AI fit + strategic impact.

Day 2: O.L.C.D Framework™ – Diagnostic Mapping

What you’ll learn: How to map problems to actual AI use cases and understand what you’d need to execute.

The framework: O.L.C.D (Outcome, Logic, Capability, Data)

  • 1 hour teaching + practice: We walk through real client examples. You’ll see how different problems map to different AI solutions (some are ML, some are automation, some are pure workflow).
  • 1 hour group breakout: You’ll take your top use case from Day 1 and map it using O.L.C.D. We’ll uncover what data you have, what you’re missing, and what’s actually feasible.
  • 30 min Q&A: Get clarity on roadblocks, data gaps, or execution challenges.

By end of Day 2: You’ll have a diagnostic blueprint for your top 1–2 use cases (what to build, what data you need, who should own it).

Day 3: ROI Mapping – Execution & Measurement

What you’ll learn: How to quantify the business impact of your AI initiative and lock in stakeholder buy-in.

The methodology: Before/After ROI Model (time saved, cost reduced, revenue enabled, risk mitigated)

  • 1 hour teaching + practice: We walk through real ROI scenarios. You’ll see how to translate “saves time” into “saves ₹X” and how to build confidence intervals for your CFO.
  • 1 hour group breakout: You’ll build the ROI model for your top use case. What’s the before state? What’s the after state? What’s the timeline? What’s the investment required?
  • 1 hour Q&A + Roadmap finalization: We’ll lock in your 30–90 day execution plan. You’ll leave with a dated, accountable roadmap.

By end of Day 3: You’ll have a ROI-backed business case and a 90-day roadmap you can hand to your team or present to leadership.
